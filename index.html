About

I am a current MD/MBA candidate at the University of Rochester interested in emerging technologies in the healthcare industry. I studied biochemistry and computer science during undergrad and want to be engaged at the intersection of these two fields. I believe there is incredible demand for innovations in the healthcare industry and want to help bring these ideas to fruition. I did my undergraduate thesis on the synthesis and functionalization of gold nanoparticles and have experience processing and analyzing next-gen sequencing data with tools such as Bowtie, Tophat, and Cufflinks. I am also an avid software developer with experience with html, css, ruby on rails, python, and java.
